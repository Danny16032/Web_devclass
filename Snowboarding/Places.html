Places to Snowboard

You can snowboard pretty much anywhere but the biggest ski resorts are Vail, Powder Mountain, and Whistler Blackcomb.

Vail

Vail Ski Resort is a world-class Colorado destination, one of the largest in North America, offering over 5,300 acres of diverse terrain across its Front Side, Back Bowls, and Blue Sky Basin for all skill levels, from beginner groomers to advanced powder glades, complemented by a European-style village with extensive dining, shopping, and luxury amenities, making it a premier, accessible ski vacation spot with excellent infrastructure and legendary bowls like China Bowl.

Powder mountian

Powder Mountain (Pow Mow) in Eden, Utah, is North Americas largest ski resort by acreage, famous for abundant natural snow, vast terrain, and unparalleled powder, all while limiting daily skier numbers for an uncrowded experience. It offers diverse terrain from beginner to expert, including lift-served, bus-serviced, hike-to, and snowcat-accessed areas, focusing on authentic skiing and snowboarding away from the masses, with unique features like night skiing and extensive backcountry options.

Whistler Blackcomb

Whistler Blackcomb is North America's largest ski resort, located in British Columbia, Canada, featuring two massive mountains (Whistler & Blackcomb) connected by the record-breaking PEAK 2 PEAK Gondola, offering vast terrain for all skill levels, world-class amenities, and year-round activities like mountain biking and hiking, making it a premier destination for winter sports enthusiasts and tourists.
