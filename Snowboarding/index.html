Snowboard Basics

Snowboarding basics involve learning to balance on one foot (the "skate" stance), control speed and stop using your heel and toe edges (pressure on heels to stop, toes to go), and make gradual traversing turns across the slope instead of straight down, all while maintaining a stable, slightly bent-knee stance with your hips stacked over your edges for balance and control. Key equipment includes properly fitting boots, a helmet, and wrist guards for protection, with lessons recommended for faster skill development.

Where To Get The Gear

There are many different stores you can go to to get snowboarding gear. If you are at a resort you may rent equipment there. You can also go to a ski shop and get equipment there. There are many other options online that you can look at.
